Continuing Recorders and Music
Instructor: EllenAnn Chiddix-Johnston
Ages: 6 - 18
Maximum class size: 15
$170
Come join us in continuing to play soprano recorder! This second introductory class will help further students’ understanding of music: beat, rhythm, dynamics, and time signatures, plus much more. Students will continue learning to read treble clef music, concepts of melody and harmony, as well as musical forms including Rounds, Call and Response, Duets, Waltzes, and be exposed to different cultural forms of music. Prerequisites include previous recorder class or ability to read basic treble clef.
